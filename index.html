Congratulations on the successful XI World Congress!

The 2016 XI ISUD World Congress was held to commemorate the 28th anniversary of the Society's establishment in Warsaw. The Congress brought together scholars from 39 countries spanning North and South America, Europe, Asia, Africa, and the Pacific Islands, showcasing a diverse range of cultures and perspectives. The event was attended by approximately 145 scholars, who presented original research. Throughout the Congress, there was a prevalent sense of goodwill and a strong desire to promote intercultural philosophical discourse, creating a positive and collaborative atmosphere.
