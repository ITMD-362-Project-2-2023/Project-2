The International Society for Universal Dialogue (ISUD) comprises scholars, particularly philosophers, from various regions of the globe, who are committed to fostering global discourse and exchanging ideas on essential issues that impact all human beings. These include topics like world peace, human rights, cultural diversity, and the preservation of the environment. The society aims to promote intercultural understanding and to encourage conversation and collaboration to address these universal concerns.

  • What role does philosophical thinking have to play in moving toward a more rational and more humane future?
  • What are the ethical principles of dialoque?
  • How may one understand cultural diversity and university?
  • How best to work toward a culture of peace?
  • What is the relationship between the basic liberties of individual and the collective rightsd of cultural groups?
